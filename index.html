Fields & Waves Lab

The Fields & Waves Lab at the University of Sannio works at the boundary of physics and engineering. We study fundamental physical aspects of wave propagation in complex media and scenarios, and explore engineering applications to communications and sensing.

We are especially interested in metamaterials, aperiodic order, ray chaos, and gravitational interferometry.
